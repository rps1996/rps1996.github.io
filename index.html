Passionate | Focused | Creative

"The place to improve the world is first in one's own heart and head and hands, and then work outward from there."

French-American dual national. Interested in how business and design can address real world issues and spark cross-cultural communication. I love connecting with people of a diversity of backgrounds and interests. Committed to always challenge myself by picking up new skills and pursuing diverse opportunities, wherever they may take me.

A former ballet dancer, I currently live in New York city. You can usually find me on my yoga mat at your local yoga studio or at the Hungarian Pastry Shop reading a good book as I pick apart a delicious croissant. I really enjoy walking - everything from urban trekking to wilderness backpacking, playing guitar and generally having a good time with my friends.

As an undergrad at Columbia University I am studying Architecture and East Asian Languages and Cultures. I am pursuing architecture as a means to both ground myself in conceptual thinking and explore my interest in urban design. I am really interested in the history and culture of China, and am working towards fluency in Mandarin. Some of my other courses have also focused on sustainability, another area of interest.
